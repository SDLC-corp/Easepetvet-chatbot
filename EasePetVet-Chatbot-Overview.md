# EasePetVet Website Chatbot

### A friendly assistant that answers visitor questions on your website, day and night.

---

## What it is

The EasePetVet chatbot is a smart assistant that lives on your website. When a
visitor has a question, they simply type it into a small chat window and get an
instant, helpful answer.

What makes it special: the chatbot answers **only** using information from your
own website. It reads and understands every page of easepetvet.com, so its
answers are always accurate and on-brand. If it doesn't know something, it says
so honestly rather than making up an answer.

It works around the clock, never gets tired, and can help many visitors at the
same time.

---

## What it does for your business

- **Answers questions instantly** — visitors get help in seconds, at any hour,
  without waiting for a staff member.
- **Captures new leads** — visitors can leave their name, email, and phone
  number, so your team can follow up with interested pet parents and vets.
- **Saves staff time** — common, repetitive questions are handled automatically,
  freeing your team for the conversations that really need a person.
- **Keeps every answer accurate** — because it only uses your website, it never
  shares wrong or off-brand information.
- **Gives you insight** — every conversation is saved so you can see what
  visitors are asking about most.

---

## How a conversation works

1. A visitor clicks the chat button in the corner of the website.
2. They tell the chatbot whether they're a **pet parent** or a **vet**, so the
   answers fit their needs.
3. They can optionally share their **name, email, and phone** if they'd like the
   team to follow up.
4. They ask their question in plain, everyday language.
5. The chatbot finds the most relevant information on your website and replies
   with a clear answer, often including a link to the page where they can read
   more.
6. The conversation continues naturally — they can ask follow-up questions.

## Built-in trust and safety

- **No made-up answers.** The chatbot will never invent prices, services, or
  facts. If the information isn't on your website, it politely says it can't help
  with that and points the visitor to contact the team.
- **Stays on topic.** If someone asks something unrelated to EasePetVet, the
  chatbot gently steers away rather than giving an irrelevant answer.
- **Polite limits.** Each conversation has a sensible length. When a visitor
  reaches it, the chatbot shares a support email so they can reach a real person.
- **Respectful of contact details.** Visitor information is stored securely and
  only used so your team can follow up.

---

## Keeping it up to date

Your website changes over time — new services, new prices, new pages. The
chatbot can **refresh its knowledge** of the website so its answers always match
what's currently published.

- This refresh can be run on demand whenever you make important updates.
- It can also be scheduled to update automatically on a regular basis (for
  example, around once a month), with no manual effort.

---

## The team dashboard

Your team gets a private, password-protected dashboard to manage the chatbot and
see how it's performing.

From the dashboard your team can:

- **Read past conversations** — see exactly what visitors asked and how the
  chatbot replied.
- **Search and filter** — find chats by date or by audience (pet parents vs.
  vets).
- **View at-a-glance stats** — understand overall activity and interest.
- **Refresh the website knowledge** — update what the chatbot knows with a single
  click.

---

## What's needed to run it

- The chatbot connects to your existing website — no redesign required.
- It's added with a small snippet, similar to how other website tools (like chat
  or analytics widgets) are installed.
- It runs quietly in the background and is designed to be low-maintenance.
