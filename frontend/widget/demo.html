Ease Pet Vet — Chatbot Widget Demo

This page loads the embeddable widget the same way a real website would. The floating assistant appears in the bottom-right corner. Open it and try: "How much do I need to pay?", "Can Ease help my dog with anxiety?", "Do vets have to pay anything?", or "asdf nonsense".

Embed snippet

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://YOUR_DOMAIN/widget/ease-chatbot-widget.css">
<script
  src="https://YOUR_DOMAIN/widget/ease-chatbot-widget.js"
  data-api-base-url="https://YOUR_BACKEND_DOMAIN"
  data-position="bottom-right"
  data-theme="light"
  defer>
</script>

This demo points the widget at http://localhost:3000 via the config below.
